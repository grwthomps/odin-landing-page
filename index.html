City Zoo needs your help.
Every year, millions of visitors come to City Zoo to see animals like the red fox pictured here. While caring for our animals that visitors see every day is our top priority, there are many other activities that are just as vital. Whether it's public educational programs or protecting vital habitats, none of these efforts would be possible without your contributions. Now, more than ever, animals around the world rely on our education, care, conservation, and research for their survival. Please consider donating today.

How Your Donation is Used:
Education of the public through fundraisers, advertising, and attractions.
Care for the animals within our zoo, as well as providing assistance to animals at other zoos and facilities.
Conservation of wild animal habitats and, where possible, habitat expansion.
Research to learn more about these wonderful creatures in order to serve them better.
